Past Talks and Workshops

Last modified: 2022-01-05

I have spoken on reproducible research and open source collaboration at high profile software conferences in the UK and globally for virtual conferences. I also lead workshops and tutorials on Binder for all ranges of abilities both in-person and remotely.

I am always happy to attend and speak at conferences, and I really enjoy sharing my knowledge with new audiences. I am grateful to all the conference organisers who have done a wonderful job hosting me so far. üëê
